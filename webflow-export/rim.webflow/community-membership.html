Become a Member

‍RIM is a refuge we create together—a place for learning, practice, and genuine friendship. Everyone is welcome, from all backgrounds and phases of life. Come as you are.

Community Care Agreements

We ask members to hold these four shared intentions, which together create a safe and supportive environment for all.

  1. ‍Care for Yourself
    Take responsibility for your own path. Teachers and community offer support and friendship, but the journey is yours to walk.‍
  2. Care for Others
    ‍    Show up for one another. Your presence and goodwill are gifts to every member of this community.
  3. Care for RIM
    ‍    RIM is 100% community-funded. We ask that all members contribute financially in a way that feels right to them.‍
  4. Care for Our Shared Vision
    ‍    We practice to cultivate wisdom and compassion—for ourselves, each other, and all beings.
